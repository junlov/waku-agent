★ Hero moment 1 — the retrieval gate

Most assistants hit their memory on every turn — slow, and worse, irrelevant memories bias the answer. Here a cheap model first decides does this turn even need memory? “what’s 2+2” → skip. “when am I meeting Alex?” → retrieve. Latency and bias, saved.

★ Hero moment 2 — two kinds of eval

“Did the right tool fire?” is a unit test — 0 or 1, no model judges it. “Was the reply good?” is an LLM-as-judge score with a threshold. Teams constantly conflate them; here they’re separate suites. See architecture.md for the exact release-gate contract.
